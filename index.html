Volta-Ho, Ghana

Ho is the capital of the Ho Municipal District and the Volta Region of Ghana. The city is the capital of unrecognised Western Togoland. The Municipality shares boundaries with Adaklu and Agotime-Ziope Districts to the South, Ho West District to the North and West and the Republic of Togo to the East. Its total land area is 2,361 square kilometers (912 sq mi) thus representing 11.5 percent of the region's total land area.
