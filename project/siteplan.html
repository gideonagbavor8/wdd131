Student Access Hub - Site Plan

Site Name

Student Access Hub

The name was selected to reflect the core functionality of the portal, providing students with easy access to all their academic information and resources.

Site Purpose

The site aims to provide students with a centralized platform to manage their academic responsibilities, access study materials, and engage in discussions with peers. It will offer grades, assignments, class schedules, study resources, and discussion forums.

Scenarios

  • How can I check my grades for this semester?
  • What assignments are due next week and what are their deadlines?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #3B5998 (Navy Blue) - Used for the navigation bar, headings, and important buttons.

Secondary Color: #F7F7F7 (Light Gray) - Used for the background, paragraphs, and accents.

Typography

Heading Font: "Roboto", sans-serif - Used for all headings (h1, h2, h3, etc.).

Body Font: "Open Sans", sans-serif - Used for body text and paragraphs.
